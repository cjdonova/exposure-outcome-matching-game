Exposure & Outcome Matching Game

Exposure: In public health research, an exposure is a factor or characteristic that might influence health. Examples: age, access to healthy food, behaviors, or environment.

Outcome: An outcome is what is measured to see if there is a difference between groups. Examples: rates of disease, health behaviors, or quality of sleep.
Instructions: Click two cards to reveal them. Match an Exposure with its correct Outcome. If the pair is correct, they stay revealed. Try to find all matches!
